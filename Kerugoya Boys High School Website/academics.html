Competency-Based Education (CBC) — 2026

Kerugoya Boys Senior School is fully geared up for the Competency-Based Education rollout. Grade 9 learners seeking placement to Grade 10 can now apply through the Ministry of Education portal. We offer 30 subject combinations across all three pathways.

01
Choose Your Pathway
Select STEM, Social Sciences, or Arts & Sports Science based on your career interests.
02
Select Subject Combinations
Pick 3 subject combinations within your chosen pathway from our 30 available options.
03
Apply via MoE Portal
Visit selection.education.go.ke to select schools and confirm your placement.
