How to Join
Admission Pathways
Kerugoya Boys Senior School accepts students through the Ministry of Education placement system and direct school applications.
🏛️

Government Placement (MoE)

As a public Extra-County school, the primary admission route for Grade 10 is through the Ministry of Education's online selection portal for CBC learners completing Grade 9.

  • Complete Grade 9 in any accredited junior school
  • Select Kerugoya Boys on the MoE CBC portal
  • Choose your pathway and subject combinations
  • Placement is merit-based on available vacancies
  • Visit: selection.education.go.ke
📋

Direct School Inquiry

Parents and guardians seeking additional guidance on admissions, fees, or placement procedures can contact the school directly for clarification and assistance.

  • Call the Principal directly: 0716 337 026
  • Email: kerugoyaboys2015@gmail.com
  • Visit the school in person for a guided tour
  • Vacancies available on competitive basis
  • School located 1km from Kerugoya town on Kerugoya-Kutus road
Process
Admission Steps
1
Complete Grade 9
Successfully complete your junior school education under CBC.
2
Choose Pathway
Select STEM, Social Sciences, or Arts & Sports on the MoE portal.
3
Select School
Add Kerugoya Boys Senior School to your 4 school preferences per combination.
4
Receive Placement
Await MoE placement confirmation and report to school on the designated date.
