Home Away From Home

A Thriving Boarding Community

Kerugoya Boys is a full boarding school set on a 50-acre campus in Kirinyaga County. Our dormitories provide a safe, structured, and supportive environment where students build lifelong friendships and strong character.

From morning preps to evening study halls, every hour of the day is purposefully structured to maximize academic and personal growth.

Safe DormitoriesSecure, well-maintained sleeping quarters

Study PrepsStructured evening prep sessions
